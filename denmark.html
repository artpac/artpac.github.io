DENMARK


Denmark is one of my favorite countries. The colorful streets of Copenhagen capture your attention and the view is perfect for bringing a camera around the city. The canals, pastries, and museums are something you wouldn't want to miss if you're traveling in the area. Of the countries I've been to, this is the most peaceful in atmosphere. Even though it was a short trip, it showed a lot of character. Denmark is perfect for nature lovers as well. With its unique blend of history, culture, and natural wonders, Denmark is definitely up there on the list to visit.




Copenhagen

Copenhagen is a city that feels surprisingly like home. From the colorful houses of Nyhavn to the towers of Rosenborg Castle, every street corner is worth photographing. Walking along the waterfront, you can see the famous Little Mermaid statue, if you can manage to see past the crowds. Tivoli Gardens was much more impressive than I expected as well. Copenhagen was very similar to Amsterdam in appearance, but completely different in atmosphere. The cafes were lovely and I really enjoyed my time.
